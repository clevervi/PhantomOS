# Privacy Policy for PhantomOS

**PhantomOS** is designed with a "Privacy First, Local Always" philosophy. 

## 1. Zero Telemetry
PhantomOS **does not collect** any diagnostic data, usage statistics, or telemetry from your machine. No data is ever sent to our servers.

## 2. Local-Only Execution
All system modifications, analysis, and processing happen entirely on your local machine. PhantomOS does not require an internet connection to operate its core features.

## 3. Transparency
Because PhantomOS is open-source, you can verify exactly what the code does by inspecting the repository.

## 4. Third-Party Tools
PhantomOS may integrate with industry-standard security tools like Seatbelt. When these tools are executed, they run locally and their output is only visible to you within the application.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at **privacy@clevervi.com**.
